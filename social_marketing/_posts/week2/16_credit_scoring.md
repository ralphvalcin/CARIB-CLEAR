## Post Content

CreditProfileGenerator is live.

It scores MSMEs on the 5 C's of credit — Character, Capacity, Capital, Conditions, Collateral — but with a twist: Collateral defaults to zero.

Collateral-based lending locks out 80-90% of Caribbean businesses. They don't have real estate. But they have cash flow — POS receipts, invoices, mobile money transactions.

The agent connects that data, builds a cash-flow profile, and assigns a credit rating (AAA down to D). No land title required.
