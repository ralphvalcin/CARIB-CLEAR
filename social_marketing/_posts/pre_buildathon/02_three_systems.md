## Thread Content

**1/4**
CARIB-CLEAR isn't built from scratch. It's three systems I already built, merged into one. Here's what each brings:

**2/4**
**The trading system.**

I spent months building an algorithmic trading platform with governance, human-in-the-loop approval, broker abstraction (paper/live), and a multi-asset matching engine.

That governance layer? It ports straight to FX settlement. That broker abstraction? It's now Stellar, ACH, Mobile Money rails.

**3/4**
**JARVIS — the voice agent platform.**

Whisper for speech-to-text. Ollama for LLM. Kokoro for TTS. VAD and interruption handling. Streaming responses.

It lets merchants deposit, check balances, and request loans — by voice, in Kreyol, English, French, Spanish. No app needed.

**4/4**
**Kreyol-AI — the first open-source Haitian Creole LLM.**

QLoRA fine-tuned Llama 3.1-8B. Built for a language with 12M speakers and almost zero AI support.

Most of Haiti runs on mobile money (MonCash). Most users aren't literate in English or French. Voice + Kreyol unlocks the biggest remittance corridor in the Caribbean ($3.8B/yr).

Three codebases. One system. One mission.
