## Post Content

I'm building CARIB-CLEAR for the Future Caribbean Global AI Buildathon (Track 3: Finance, Payments & MSME Capital).

Why?

I'm Haitian. Remittances are 25% of Haiti's GDP — $3.8B a year flowing through a system that takes 7-9% in fees and 2-3 days to settle. A hotel in Barbados paying a supplier in Jamaica routes BBD → USD → JMD. That's three currency conversions, two correspondent banks, and a week of waiting — for a payment between two Caribbean islands.

80-90% of Caribbean businesses are MSMEs. They can't get credit because they don't have real estate to offer as collateral. But they have cash flow — POS receipts, invoices, mobile money transactions. That data exists. Nobody's connecting it.

**What I'm building:**

A two-layer agentic system:

Layer 1 — CARICOM FX Swap Network
- Direct BBD↔JMD↔TTD↔XCD↔HTG matching (no USD bridge)
- Multilateral netting (80%+ volume reduction)
- Multi-rail settlement: Stellar (5s), ACH (1-3h), Mobile Money (10s)
- <1% fees, <5 min finality

Layer 2 — MSME Credit Layer
- Cash-flow scoring (no collateral)
- Integrated with regional lenders: Barita, JMMB, IDB Invest
- Voice-first UI in Kreyol, English, French, Spanish via JARVIS

**What makes this different:**

Not a prototype. Three proven codebases merged:
- A trading system with production governance/HITL/approval queue
- JARVIS voice agent platform (Whisper, Ollama, streaming TTS, interruption)
- Kreyol-AI — the first open-source Haitian Creole LLM

21 days on NVIDIA H200. One developer. Advisors from Barita, WFP, Infolytics.

If you're a Caribbean payments/compliance engineer or a multilingual voice/LLM engineer, I'm recruiting. DM me.
