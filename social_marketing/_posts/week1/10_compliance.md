## Post Content

ComplianceAgent went in today.

5 regulators (BOJ, CBB, CBTT, BRH, ECCB). 5 different KYC document requirements. 5 different AML thresholds. 5 different sanctions lists.

One agent that knows which rules apply to which transaction, screens every party, and escalates to a human when it hits a threshold.

The Caribbean isn't one market with one rulebook. The agent handles that — not the user.
