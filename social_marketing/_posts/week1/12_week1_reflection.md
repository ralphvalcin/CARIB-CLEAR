Week 1 down. Here's what I learned building a fintech infrastructure solo with AI agents.

**2/4**
The agents are the easy part.

The hard part is the governance — who approves what, when do we escalate to a human, what happens if two agents disagree. I spent more time on the approval queue than on any matching algorithm. That's probably right.

**3/4**
Also learned: the abstraction layer matters more than the implementation.

I built MultiRailBroker with Stellar first. Took a day. The ACH adapter took an hour more. Mobile Money took 30 minutes. Because the interface was right the first time.

Get the interface right and the rest flows.

**4/4**
Week 2: multi-rail routing + voice. JARVIS speaks finance now. Let's see how that goes.
