## Post Content

FlowVisibilityAgent is live.

It ingests currency flow signals — demand and supply — across 5 Caribbean jurisdictions (Jamaica, Barbados, Trinidad, Haiti, ECCB). Each signal carries: currency, direction (buy/sell), amount, urgency, source type.

Think of it as a radar for where capital wants to move.

Next step: feed this into P2PMatchingEngine so it can find direct swaps without touching USD.
