## Post Content

NetSettlementAgent: the boring but beautiful part.

A owes B. B owes C. C owes A. In a traditional system, that's three separate settlements moving real money every time.

Netting collapses them. A pays B the difference. B pays C. C pays A. Gross volume drops 80-95%. Less capital trapped in transit. Fewer fees. Faster finality.

CLS settles $6T/day this way. CARIB-CLEAR does it for Caribbean currencies.
