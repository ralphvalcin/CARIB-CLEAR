## 🎯 Voice & Positioning

**Who you are:**
> Haitian native. 4-5 years in tech. Building alone with AI tools. Three codebases merged into one. Personal mission.

**Why people should care:**
> This isn't another fintech demo. This is a Haitian engineer building the financial rails his country needs — agents that settle FX without touching USD, cash-flow lending for MSMEs with no collateral, all in Kreyol.

**Tone:**
- Short sentences. Specifics over hype.
- "I built X. Here's why it matters."
- Acknowledge the hard parts (buildathons ARE hard)
- Narrative first, technical detail second
- No corporate speak. You're a builder, not a press release.
